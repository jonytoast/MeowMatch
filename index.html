Why Adopt?

"Adopt, Don't Shop" is our mission statement, and here's why we believe in it:

There's A LOT of cats waiting to be adopted around you ...

You think breeders are the way to go, but ...
breeders cost a lot more, while animal shelters offer a variety of breeds for you to select from for your new cat

Most important of all ...
Over 920,000 shelter animals are euthanized each year
